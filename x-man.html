About

"X-Men" is a popular superhero franchise that originated in comic books published by Marvel Comics. Created by writer Stan Lee and artist Jack Kirby, the X-Men made their debut in "The X-Men #1" in 1963. The series follows a group of mutants, individuals born with superhuman abilities, who are led by Professor Charles Xavier. The mutants, often feared and persecuted by humanity due to their powers, are taught to control and use their abilities for the betterment of mankind.
